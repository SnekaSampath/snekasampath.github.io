My Leadership Philosophy



Cultivating a People-Centric Culture

I firmly believe in placing the team at the forefront. My primary mission is to empower the team, providing them the autonomy to explore, experiment, and learn. By offering autonomy alongside a demand for accountability, I foster an environment that nurtures well-rounded UX practitioners.

Pick Your Battles

When user requirements clash with business needs, flexibility becomes important to find common ground. While prioritizing the user experience is important, there are times when nurturing stakeholder relationships outweighs immediate design benefits. Distinguishing between these two situations is key in establishing long-term partnerships with the business.







Discovery Before Design

Incorporating collaborative design, particularly during the initial phases of a project, facilitates rapid alignment of project goals, minimizes subpar design iterations, and streamlines the design process in the long run.

Data Is King

Data is the key element that transforms opinions into indisputable facts. Insights learned from design standards, behavioral economics, external research, and user testing can give the team the confidence to make informed decisions. My guiding principle is simple: “When in doubt, gather actionable data through testing!"
